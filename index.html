Conclusion

The Same Sea, Different Shores

Whether fleeing on overcrowded boats in 1979 or walking across borders in 2024, refugees share one truth: they are not defined by what they fled from, but by what they build.

Solidarity Works
Vietnamese resettlement succeeded because of will.

Answer: Both crises share forced displacement but differ in scale and outcomes. The lesson: when the world commits to resettlement, refugees become one of the greatest investments a nation can make.
